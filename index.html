Who we are

Wellness that fits the student life

WellSphere is a student-led organization at Asia Pacific University (APU) on a mission to make healthy living simple, social, and stigma-free.

Between deadlines, part-time jobs, and late-night study sessions, wellbeing is often the first thing students drop. We're here to change that — with practical tools, welcoming events, and a community that has your back. Everything we build is free and open to students everywhere.

  • Evidence-based tips designed for busy students
  • Judgement-free mental health support & resources
  • Fun events that actually fit your timetable
