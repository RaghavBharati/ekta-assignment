How Healthy Is Your Student Lifestyle?

Answer 5 quick questions for your personalised wellness score.

1. How many hours do you sleep on a typical night?

2. How often do you exercise or do at least 30 minutes of activity?

3. How many servings of fruit & vegetables do you eat per day?

4. How would you describe your usual stress level?

5. How much water do you drink on a typical day?
