Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about WellSphere.

About WellSphere & Membership

WellSphere is a student-led health and wellness community at Asia Pacific University. We bring together fitness sessions, mental-wellness support, nutrition guidance and interactive wellness tools in one friendly place, so building healthy habits feels social rather than lonely.

Everyone is welcome — students, staff and even curious first-timers who just want to feel a little healthier. There is no fitness level, background or experience required; if you care about your wellbeing, you already belong here.

Yes, joining WellSphere is completely free. As a student organisation, our goal is to make wellness accessible to all, so there are no fees to become a member or to access the core programs and tools.

WellSphere is based at APU and most of our members are APU students and staff, but many of our online resources, articles and tools are open to anyone. Some on-campus events may be limited to the university community for safety and space reasons — the event details will always make this clear.

Head to our Join Us page and fill in the short membership form — it only takes a minute. Once you are in, you can register for events, use the wellness dashboard and get our weekly tips straight to your inbox.

Absolutely — complete beginners are our favourite people to welcome. Our sessions are designed with gentle, scalable options, and our friendly members are always happy to help you find a comfortable starting point at your own pace.

Events & Programs

The vast majority of our events and programs are free for members. On the rare occasion an activity involves an external cost — such as a venue hire or special equipment — we will state this clearly on the event page beforehand.

Visit our Events page, choose the session you like and follow the registration link. You will get a confirmation, and popular events fill up quickly, so it is worth signing up early to secure your spot.

No problem at all — plans change and we understand. Please cancel your registration if you can so we can offer your place to someone on the waitlist; simply let us know through the contact form or your confirmation email.

Yes, please do — WellSphere grows from student ideas. Whether it is a hiking trip, a cooking demo or a study-stress workshop, drop us your suggestion via the contact page and our team will genuinely consider it for the next calendar.

Yes, we run a mix of in-person and online sessions so you can take part from anywhere. Guided meditations, webinars and virtual workshops are regularly scheduled, and each event listing shows whether it is on campus, online or both.

For most sessions you just need comfortable clothing and a water bottle. If a particular activity needs anything extra — like a yoga mat or running shoes — we will list it in the event details, and we can often lend basics to members who need them.

Wellness Tools & Privacy

The wellness dashboard lets you track simple daily habits like water, sleep, mood and activity, then shows your progress over time. It is designed to be quick and encouraging — a gentle nudge toward healthier routines rather than a strict tracker.

Your dashboard entries stay right in your own browser using local storage — they are never uploaded to a server or shared with us. This means your information is private to your device; clearing your browser data or switching devices will reset it.

No — our calculators and tools offer general guidance for educational purposes only, and are not a substitute for professional medical advice. If you have specific health concerns, please speak with a qualified doctor or healthcare provider.

WellSphere offers peer support and wellbeing resources, but we are not a crisis service. For professional help, please reach out to APU's counselling services or a licensed provider, and in an emergency contact local emergency services immediately — our Mental Wellness page lists helpful links.

The wellness quiz is a light self-reflection tool meant to spark awareness of your habits, not a clinical assessment. Treat your results as a friendly starting point for conversation and gentle goal-setting rather than a precise diagnosis.

Never — we send a single weekly email with genuinely useful wellness tips and event reminders, and nothing more. You can unsubscribe at any time with one click, and we never sell or share your email address.
