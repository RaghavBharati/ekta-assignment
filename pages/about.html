About WellSphere

We're a student-led community at APU on a simple mission — to make wellbeing feel doable, social, and genuinely part of student life.

Our Story

Started by students, built for students

WellSphere began with a small idea and a big frustration — that staying healthy at university felt like something you had to figure out completely on your own.

Back in 2022, a handful of APU students noticed the same pattern everywhere: deadlines piling up, skipped meals, all-nighters, and nobody really talking about how to cope. Gyms felt intimidating, wellbeing services felt distant, and "self-care" sounded like something for other people with more free time.

So we started small — a weekly walk-and-talk, a shared spreadsheet of cheap healthy recipes, and a group chat where you could admit you were struggling without judgment. Word spread fast. What began as a dozen friends turned into a movement of hundreds of students who believe that looking after yourself shouldn't be a solo project.

Today, WellSphere runs fitness sessions, mental-wellness circles, nutrition workshops, and campus-wide events — all designed by students who get what university life actually feels like. We're not experts lecturing from above. We're peers, walking the same path, one small habit at a time.

2022

The first spark

A few APU students launch a casual weekly walk-and-talk and a shared healthy-recipe folder. No branding, no committee — just people trying to feel a little better together.

2023

Becoming official

WellSphere is formally recognised as an APU student organization. We elect our first committee, run our launch fair, and welcome over 150 members in a single semester.

2024

Three pillars take shape

We structure our programs around Fitness, Mental Wellness, and Nutrition, hosting our first mindfulness retreat and partnering with campus counselling services.

2026

A campus-wide community

With a growing digital dashboard, monthly events, and a member base spanning every faculty, WellSphere is now one of APU's most active wellbeing communities — and we're just getting started.

What Drives Us

Mission, vision & objectives

Everything we do points back to one belief — that wellbeing is a skill every student can learn, practise, and share.

🎯

Our Mission

To make healthy living accessible, social, and stigma-free for every APU student — meeting people exactly where they are and helping them build small habits that actually stick through the chaos of university life.

🔭

Our Vision

A campus where looking after your body and mind is completely normal — where no student feels alone in their struggles, and where wellbeing is woven into the everyday fabric of student life, not squeezed in as an afterthought.

✅

Our Objectives

  • Run inclusive, free wellness programs every week
  • Break the stigma around mental health on campus
  • Connect students to trusted support and resources
  • Grow a peer network that looks out for one another
Core Values

The principles behind everything we do

These six values guide how we show up — for our members, for each other, and for the wider APU community.

⚖️

Balance

Grades matter, but so do you. We champion a healthier rhythm between study, rest, and play.

🤝

Community

No one thrives alone. We build real connections where students genuinely support one another.

🌱

Growth

Progress over perfection. Every small step forward is worth celebrating, no matter how tiny.

💚

Inclusivity

Every background, ability, and fitness level is welcome here — always, no exceptions.

🧘

Wellbeing

Mind and body together. We treat mental and physical health as two halves of one whole.

✨

Empowerment

We give students the tools, knowledge, and confidence to take charge of their own health.
